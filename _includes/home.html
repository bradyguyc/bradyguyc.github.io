How it Started

Being an avid reader of book series I was using a website called fictfact to keep track of my reading for books in a series. It was a great website provided exactly what was needed no more no less, until it didn't exist anymore...'

The Vision

A mobile app that allows you to create a collection of book series where you can track what you have read and what's in the queue by series. Allowing you to maintain a list of the next books to read across all the series in your collection.'

The Big Picture

There is a fair amount of tech involved here and my journal attempts to document my journey. Utilizing Microsoft: Visual Studio with Xamarin, Azure, B2C, CosmosDB, App Center, along with google's book api, dbpedia knowledge graph and other tools take a look at how it all fits together.'
